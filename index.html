We are an innovation platform discovering, supporting, and scaling solutions for Africa’s most critical food system challenges.

Who We Are

An Ecosystem Built to Scale Impact.

BioNovaa is a food systems innovation platform focused on discovering, developing, and supporting solutions that improve how Africa produces, distributes, and consumes food.

We exist to turn ideas into real solutions that impact real food systems, operating at the powerful intersection of:

Innovation • Entrepreneurship • Research • Technology • Sustainability • Collaboration

Our Vision

A future where Africa’s food systems are resilient, sustainable, and innovation-driven.

Our Mission

To discover, support, and scale innovation that improves Africa’s food systems.

A shift is possible. BioNovaa was created as part of that shift.

Founder's Perspective

BioNovaa began from a personal conviction. As someone standing at the intersection of food science, technology, software engineering, and data analysis, I have seen both the depth of the problems and the scale of the opportunity.

I am still learning. I am still building. I am still figuring things out. But I am certain of one thing:

Africa’s food systems will not transform by accident. They will transform because people choose to build the systems that enable change.

BioNovaa is one of those systems. It is not built all at once. It is being built step by step, through experimentation, learning, and continuous improvement.

This is not just a project. It is infrastructure in progress.

The Challenge

Africa’s Food Systems Face Structural Challenges

Across production, processing, distribution, nutrition, and sustainability, key gaps persist in the ecosystem.

Post-harvest losses
Limited value addition
Weak market linkages
Low adoption of innovation
Fragmented ecosystems
Limited early-stage support

Yet, Africa has the talent, ideas, and potential.

What’s missing is structured support to discover and scale solutions.
That is where BioNovaa comes in.

Our Approach

Four Pillars of Transformation

BioNovaa focuses on a structured pipeline to turn potential into impact.

1. Discover

We identify emerging innovators, ideas, and opportunities across Africa’s food systems through innovation challenges and open calls.

2. Develop

We support innovators through mentorship, exposure, and structured guidance, connecting them with experts to strengthen their ideas.

3. Support

We help promising solutions move beyond ideas toward real implementation via ecosystem connections and long-term support pathways.

4. Build Ecosystem

We bring together innovators, experts, institutions, and partners to strengthen the innovation landscape. BioNovaa serves as a catalyst.

Continuous Innovation

Sustaining the Momentum

Innovation doesn't stop at ideation. BioNovaa provides a suite of continuous programs designed to nurture, validate, and scale emerging solutions.

Innovation Sprint Labs

Short, structured sessions where teams refine ideas, validate assumptions, and improve feasibility.

Continuation Support

Helping teams continue development instead of stopping at early ideation.

Debate Series

Structured discussions exploring critical food systems questions, bringing together innovators and experts.

Review & Feedback

Providing direct, actionable feedback from experienced mentors and practitioners.

Founder Check-Ins

Supporting innovators continuously through the tough early stages of development.

Knowledge Sessions

Sharing practical insights on building solutions within real food systems constraints.
