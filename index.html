Who We Are

The Challenge

Africa’s Food Systems Face Structural Challenges

Across production, processing, distribution, nutrition, and sustainability, key gaps persist in the ecosystem.

Post-harvest losses
Limited value addition
Weak market linkages
Low adoption of innovation
Fragmented ecosystems
Limited early-stage support

Our Approach

Building a Scalable Innovation Pipeline

BioNovAA focuses on execution through a structured pathway that helps ideas move from concept to real-world implementation.

1. Discover

Identifying emerging innovators, ideas, and opportunities through challenges and open calls.

2. Develop

Supporting innovators through mentorship and connecting them with industry experts.

3. Support

Moving beyond ideas toward real implementation via ecosystem connections and long-term support.

4. Build Ecosystem

Bringing together innovators, researchers, and partners to strengthen the landscape.
